# HiveMem Licensing

HiveMem is **fair-code** licensed under the [Sustainable Use License](LICENSE).

Source-available, free for personal and internal use, commercially restricted. Learn more about the fair-code model at [faircode.io](https://faircode.io).

## What is fair-code?

Fair-code is a licensing model that ensures software is source-available and free for personal or internal use, but restricts commercial value extraction by third parties. It is designed to sustain open development.

## What's allowed

- Self-hosting HiveMem for personal knowledge management
- Self-hosting for your company's internal use
- Modifying source code for your own needs
- Building custom skills/agents that connect to HiveMem
- Using HiveMem in academic/research contexts
- Contributing back to the project

## What's NOT allowed

- Hosting HiveMem and charging others to access it ("HiveMem as a Service")
- White-labeling HiveMem and selling it as your own product
- Embedding HiveMem in a commercial product where HiveMem's functionality is a substantial part of the value proposition
- Reselling access to a HiveMem instance

## What about consulting?

- Building HiveMem skills or integrations for clients: **ALLOWED**
- Helping clients set up their own HiveMem instance: **ALLOWED**
- Running a managed HiveMem service for clients: **NOT ALLOWED** without a commercial agreement

## Why not MIT anymore?

HiveMem started as MIT because simplicity mattered early on. As HiveMem grows, sustainable development requires protecting against value extraction by third parties while keeping the software free and open for individuals and internal use.
